DAWN FM

Dawn FM stands as a conceptual album that highlights the idea that helplessness is a perspective rather than a definitive reality. It encapsulates the narrative journey of a character navigating the various stages of purgatory, ultimately culminating in the profound acknowledgment and embrace of their existential state.

AFTER HOURS

"After Hours" stands as a thematic album wherein Abel assumes accountability for his errors in previous relationships, all while grappling with the restraint of his self-indulgent and toxic tendencies. Comprising 14 tracks, this project unfolds as a poignant odyssey infused with both heartbreak and the influence of substances, creating a cathartic experience of discomfort where the intersection of nostalgia and responsibility takes center stage.

Release Year: 2020

Price: $39.99

MY DEAR MELANCHOLY,

My Dear Melancholy, – whose title has a comma at the end, as if Tesfaye is beginning a letter to his own sadness – isn’t an album or EP so much as the equivalent of Tesafaye standing in his ex’s garden at 3am, pissed, holding a ghettoblaster over his head and bellowing along to Chicago’s If You Leave Me Now for 22 minutes. It even ends suddenly, as if she’s finally opened her bedroom window and thrown a shoe at him.
